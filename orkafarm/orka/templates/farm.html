OUR FARMS

main goal of our foundation is to produce high quality products with our expertise and make them available to consumers efficiently. Orka also operates on the distribution network of its produce enabling customers to have best quality of its farm yield at a reasonable price. Apart from traditional supply chain management Orka has adopted modern technology which will enable customers to order Orka products sitting at home.

OrkaFarm (Orka) is a diversified agri-aqua business company focussed on producing fresh and healthy products to its customers at reasonable price. We at Orka are constantly looking for research-based solutions to improve farm productivity and also share our experiences by providing free consultancy to farmers. At Orka we believe in “Quality is the Essence”
